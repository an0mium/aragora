## FAQ

### Can I try before buying?
Yes. The Free tier includes 5 runs/month forever. Pro includes a 14-day trial.

### What happens if I exceed my run limit?
You can purchase additional runs at $2 each, or upgrade your plan.

### Is my data used for training?
Never. Your inputs and outputs are never used to train AI models.

### Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Monthly plans can be canceled anytime. Annual plans are refunded pro-rata.

### Do you offer discounts?
- **Startups:** 50% off first year (requires YC, a16z, or similar affiliation)
- **Non-profits:** 50% off all plans
- **Education:** Free Pro tier for verified .edu emails
