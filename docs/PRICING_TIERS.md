## Detailed Tier Descriptions

### Starter (Free)

Perfect for:
- Individual users exploring AI-assisted decision making
- Small projects or personal use
- Evaluating Aragora before upgrading

**Includes:**
- 100 debates per month
- Up to 3 rounds per debate
- Access to OpenRouter, Claude, and GPT-4
- REST API and SDK access
- 30-day decision history
- Community support

**Limitations:**
- Single user only
- No team collaboration features
- No chat platform integrations
- No Knowledge Mound persistence

### Pro ($49/month)

Perfect for:
- Small teams (2-10 people)
- Businesses making regular decisions
- Teams wanting chat integrations

**Includes everything in Starter, plus:**
- 1,000 debates per month
- Up to 9 rounds per debate
- 10 team members
- 3 workspaces
- Slack, Teams, Discord integration
- Knowledge Mound with cross-debate learning
- 1-year decision history
- All AI models (Gemini, DeepSeek, Qwen, Llama, local)
- Basic RBAC (Admin, Member, Viewer roles)
- MFA and 90-day audit logs
- Email support with 24h response time

**Best value for:**
- $0.049 per debate (if using all 1,000)
- Unlimited debates per workspace (pooled quota)

### Enterprise (Custom Pricing)

Perfect for:
- Large organizations (50+ people)
- Regulated industries (healthcare, finance)
- Companies requiring compliance certifications

**Includes everything in Pro, plus:**
- Unlimited debates and rounds
- Unlimited team members and workspaces
- SSO (SAML 2.0, OIDC)
- SCIM user provisioning
- Advanced RBAC with custom roles
- Unlimited audit log retention
- Field-level encryption
- SOC 2 Type II, HIPAA BAA available
- Custom data processing agreements
- Dedicated customer success manager
- Priority support (4h response)
- Custom training and onboarding
- Custom model integration
- On-premises deployment option

**Typical pricing:**
- Base: $500-2,000/month depending on scale
- Per-seat: $10-30/user/month
- Contact sales for custom quote

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Billing

**Q: Can I change tiers mid-cycle?**
A: Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

**Q: Do unused debates roll over?**
A: No, monthly quotas reset each billing cycle.

**Q: Is there an annual discount?**
A: Yes, pay annually for Pro and get 2 months free ($490/year).

### Features

**Q: Can I try Pro features before committing?**
A: Yes, we offer a 14-day Pro trial with no credit card required.

**Q: What happens to my data if I downgrade?**
A: Your data is retained according to your previous tier's limits. Excess data is archived and available for export.

**Q: Can I use my own API keys?**
A: Yes, you can bring your own LLM provider API keys to reduce costs and maintain direct billing with providers.

### Enterprise

**Q: What's included in the Enterprise SLA?**
A: 99.9% uptime guarantee, 4-hour critical issue response, and dedicated escalation path.

**Q: Can Aragora be deployed on-premises?**
A: Yes, Enterprise tier includes on-premises deployment options. Contact sales for details.

**Q: What compliance certifications are available?**
A: SOC 2 Type II (current), HIPAA BAA (available), ISO 27001 (in progress), GDPR compliant.
