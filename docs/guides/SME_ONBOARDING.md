# SME Onboarding Guide

A step-by-step guide for small and medium enterprises to get started with Aragora.

## Overview

Aragora helps SMEs make better decisions and automate business processes through multi-agent AI debates and workflows. This guide will have you up and running in under 15 minutes.

## Quick Start Paths

Choose the path that best fits your immediate needs:

| Path | Best For | Time | Key Features |
|------|----------|------|--------------|
| **Automation** | Recurring tasks | ~10 min | Invoice workflows, customer follow-ups, reporting |
| **Decisions** | Strategic choices | ~5 min | AI debates, consensus tracking, decision receipts |
| **Compliance** | Regulated industries | ~8 min | Security assessments, audit trails, policy reviews |
| **Full Setup** | Maximum value | ~15 min | All features combined |

## Onboarding Flow

### Step 1: Welcome & Account Setup

1. Sign up at [app.aragora.ai](https://app.aragora.ai)
2. Verify your email
3. Set your password

### Step 2: Organization Setup

Create your workspace:
- **Organization Name**: Your company name
- **URL Slug**: `your-company.aragora.ai`
- **Team Size**: Solo, Small (2-10), Medium (11-50), Large (50+)

### Step 3: Industry Selection

Tell us your industry for personalized templates:
- SaaS / Tech
- Professional Services
- Retail / E-commerce
- Manufacturing
- Healthcare
- Finance
- Other

### Step 4: Choose Your Path

#### Automation Path

Set up automated workflows for recurring tasks:

1. **Invoice Generation**
   - Auto-generate invoices from customer data
   - Calculate taxes and totals
   - Email delivery with PDF attachments

2. **Customer Follow-ups**
   - Identify follow-up candidates
   - Draft personalized messages
   - Schedule automated sends

3. **Inventory Alerts**
   - Monitor stock levels
   - Calculate reorder points
   - Send alerts when low

4. **Scheduled Reports**
   - Daily, weekly, or monthly reports
   - Customizable formats
   - Email delivery

#### Decisions Path

Run AI debates for better decisions:

1. **Quick Question** (2 min)
   - 3 AI agents, 2 rounds
   - Fast answers to simple questions

2. **Team Decision** (3 min)
   - 3 agents, 2 rounds
   - Collaborative decision support

3. **Architecture Review** (5 min)
   - 4 agents, 3 rounds
   - Technical decisions

4. **Vendor Evaluation** (5 min)
   - 4 agents, 3 rounds
   - Compare options objectively

#### Compliance Path

Ensure regulatory compliance:

1. **Security Assessment** (7 min)
   - 5 agents, 3 rounds
   - Comprehensive security review

2. **Policy Review** (6 min)
   - 4 agents, 3 rounds
   - Policy gap analysis

3. **Audit Preparation**
   - Decision receipts for all debates
   - Cryptographic verification
   - Full audit trails

### Step 5: Team Invites (Optional)

Invite team members by email:
- Assign roles (Admin, Member, Viewer)
- Set up approval workflows
- Enable team collaboration

### Step 6: Run Your First Task

Depending on your path:

**Automation**: Configure and run your first workflow
**Decisions**: Run your first debate with real-time streaming
**Compliance**: Complete your first security assessment

### Step 7: Review & Complete

- View your results (workflow output or debate receipt)
- Explore additional templates
- Set up recurring tasks

## SME Workflow Templates

### Invoice Generation

```yaml
Trigger: Manual or scheduled
Steps:
  1. Validate customer data
  2. Calculate line items and taxes
  3. Generate invoice document
  4. Create PDF
  5. Send via email (optional)
  6. Store record
```

**Configuration:**
- Customer data source
- Tax rates by region
- Invoice template
- Email delivery settings

### Customer Follow-up

```yaml
Trigger: Scheduled (daily/weekly)
Steps:
  1. Identify follow-up candidates
  2. Analyze customer sentiment
  3. Identify opportunities
  4. Draft personalized message
  5. Human approval checkpoint
  6. Send message
  7. Schedule next follow-up
```

**Configuration:**
- Customer segments
- Message templates
- Approval requirements
- Follow-up frequency

### Inventory Alerts

```yaml
Trigger: Scheduled (hourly/daily)
Steps:
  1. Fetch inventory levels
  2. Analyze against thresholds
  3. Calculate reorder quantities
  4. Generate alert
  5. Optional: Auto-reorder
```

**Configuration:**
- Stock thresholds
- Reorder points
- Notification channels
- Auto-reorder rules

### Scheduled Reports

```yaml
Trigger: Scheduled (daily/weekly/monthly)
Steps:
  1. Gather data
  2. Generate report
  3. Format output
  4. Send to recipients
```

**Configuration:**
- Report type
- Data sources
- Recipients
- Delivery schedule

## Best Practices for SMEs

### Starting Out

1. **Start Simple**: Begin with one workflow or debate type
2. **Iterate**: Refine based on results
3. **Document**: Keep decision receipts for important choices
4. **Automate Gradually**: Add workflows one at a time

### Team Collaboration

1. **Define Roles**: Clear ownership for workflows
2. **Set Approvals**: Human checkpoints for critical tasks
3. **Share Templates**: Standardize across team
4. **Review Together**: Weekly review of automated tasks

### Compliance & Audit

1. **Enable Receipts**: For all important debates
2. **Tag Decisions**: Categorize for easy retrieval
3. **Regular Reviews**: Monthly audit of automated actions
4. **Backup Data**: Export receipts periodically
