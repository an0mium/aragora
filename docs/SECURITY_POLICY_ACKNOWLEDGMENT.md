# Security Policy Acknowledgment

**SOC 2 Control:** CC1.1

This document establishes the formal process for employee acknowledgment of Aragora's security policies.

## Purpose

All Aragora team members must understand and acknowledge their security responsibilities. This acknowledgment is required:
- Within 30 days of joining
- Annually thereafter
- When policies are materially updated

## Security Policy Acknowledgment Statement

By signing this acknowledgment, I confirm that I have:

1. **Received and Read** the following policies:
   - Aragora Security Policy (SECURITY.md)
   - Data Classification Policy (docs/DATA_CLASSIFICATION.md)
   - Privacy Policy (docs/PRIVACY_POLICY.md)
   - Acceptable Use Policy
   - Remote Work Security Policy (docs/REMOTE_WORK_SECURITY.md)
   - Incident Response Plan (docs/INCIDENT_RESPONSE.md)

2. **Understood** my responsibilities regarding:
   - Protecting company and customer data
   - Reporting security incidents immediately
   - Using only approved tools and services
   - Following secure coding practices
   - Maintaining confidentiality of credentials

3. **Agreed** to comply with all security policies and procedures

4. **Acknowledged** that violations may result in disciplinary action

## Acknowledgment Process

### New Employees

| Step | Action | Timeline | Owner |
|------|--------|----------|-------|
| 1 | HR provides policy documents | Day 1 | HR |
| 2 | Employee completes security training | Days 1-7 | Employee |
| 3 | Employee reads all policies | Days 1-14 | Employee |
| 4 | Employee signs acknowledgment | Days 14-30 | Employee |
| 5 | Manager witnesses signature | Days 14-30 | Manager |
| 6 | HR files acknowledgment | Within 5 days | HR |

### Annual Re-acknowledgment

| Step | Action | Timeline | Owner |
|------|--------|----------|-------|
| 1 | HR sends reminder | 30 days before anniversary | HR |
| 2 | Employee reviews policy updates | Within 14 days | Employee |
| 3 | Employee completes refresher training | Within 21 days | Employee |
| 4 | Employee signs acknowledgment | By anniversary date | Employee |
| 5 | HR files acknowledgment | Within 5 days | HR |

### Policy Update Acknowledgment

| Step | Action | Timeline | Owner |
|------|--------|----------|-------|
| 1 | Policy change communicated | Upon approval | Security |
| 2 | Employee reviews changes | Within 14 days | Employee |
| 3 | Employee signs acknowledgment | Within 30 days | Employee |

## Security Training Requirements

### Initial Training (New Employees)

All new team members must complete:

| Module | Duration | Provider |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Security Awareness Fundamentals | 1 hour | Internal |
| Data Classification & Handling | 30 min | Internal |
| Phishing & Social Engineering | 30 min | Internal |
| Secure Development Practices | 1 hour | Internal (Engineering only) |
| Incident Reporting | 15 min | Internal |

### Annual Refresher Training

All team members must complete annually:

| Module | Duration | Provider |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Security Awareness Refresher | 30 min | Internal |
| Current Threats Update | 15 min | Internal |
| Policy Changes Review | 15 min | Internal |

### Role-Based Training

Additional training by role:

| Role | Additional Training | Frequency |
|------|---------------------|-----------|
| Engineering | Secure Coding (OWASP Top 10) | Annual |
| Engineering | Dependency Security | Annual |
| SRE/DevOps | Cloud Security Best Practices | Annual |
| Management | Incident Command Training | Annual |

## Specific Acknowledgments

### Access to Customer Data

Team members with access to customer data must additionally acknowledge:

```
CUSTOMER DATA ACCESS ACKNOWLEDGMENT

I understand that I have been granted access to customer data. I acknowledge:

1. I will access customer data only for legitimate business purposes
2. I will not share customer data with unauthorized parties
3. I will report any suspected data breaches immediately
4. I will follow all data handling procedures in the Data Classification Policy
5. My access will be audited and reviewed periodically

Signature: _______________________
Date: _______________________
```

### Production System Access

Team members with production system access must additionally acknowledge:

```
PRODUCTION SYSTEM ACCESS ACKNOWLEDGMENT

I understand that I have been granted access to production systems. I acknowledge:

1. I will follow all change management procedures
2. I will not make unauthorized changes to production systems
3. I will use production access only for authorized purposes
4. I will report any security incidents immediately
5. I will maintain the confidentiality of all credentials

Signature: _______________________
Date: _______________________
```

### Administrative Access

Team members with administrative access must additionally acknowledge:

```
ADMINISTRATIVE ACCESS ACKNOWLEDGMENT

I understand that I have been granted administrative access. I acknowledge:

1. I will use MFA for all administrative access
2. I will follow the principle of least privilege
3. I will audit my own access periodically
4. I will not share administrative credentials
5. I understand my actions are logged and auditable

Signature: _______________________
Date: _______________________
```

## Digital Acknowledgment Option

For remote teams, digital acknowledgment is acceptable via:

1. **Secure Form Submission**
   - Authenticated user session
   - Timestamp recorded
   - IP address logged
   - Confirmation email sent

2. **Electronic Signature**
   - DocuSign, HelloSign, or equivalent
   - Audit trail maintained
   - Certificate of completion

3. **HR System Acknowledgment**
   - Built-in policy acknowledgment module
   - Automatic tracking and reminders
   - Compliance dashboard
